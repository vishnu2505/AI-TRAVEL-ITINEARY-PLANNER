=========================================
SECTION 1: HOST CITY LOGISTICS & INSIDER TIPS
=========================================
[USA]
- Boston (Gillette Stadium)
  * Location: Foxborough, MA (29 miles/47 km from downtown).
  * Transport: MBTA "Foxboro Event Train" from South Station (Ticket required in advance).
  * Warning: Do NOT take an Uber/Lyft to the stadium; traffic is a gridlock (2+ hours). Take the train.
  * Fan Hubs: Faneuil Hall (Tourist), The North End (Italian/Soccer vibes).

- NY/NJ (MetLife Stadium)
  * Location: East Rutherford, NJ (12 miles/19 km from Manhattan).
  * Transport: NJ Transit from NY Penn Station to Secaucus Jct, transfer to Meadowlands Rail.
  * Travel Time: Allow 90 mins total due to massive crowds.
  * Fan Hubs: Times Square (Commercial), "Little Brazil" (W 46th St), Astoria (Queens) for international vibes.

- Philadelphia (Lincoln Financial)
  * Location: South Philly Sports Complex (4 miles from center).
  * Transport: SEPTA Broad Street Line (Orange) to NRG Station. Fast and easy.
  * Fan Hubs: Xfinity Live! (next to stadium), Center City (Broad St).

- Atlanta (Mercedes-Benz)
  * Location: Downtown Atlanta (0 miles).
  * Transport: MARTA Rail to GWCC/MBS Station.
  * Insider Tip: Stadium is famous for cheap food prices. Arrive early for the "Halo Board" show.
  * Fan Hubs: Centennial Olympic Park.

- Miami (Hard Rock Stadium)
  * Location: Miami Gardens (15 miles north of Downtown/South Beach).
  * Transport: NO TRAIN. Must use official shuttles or Rideshare (Expensive).
  * Warning: Traffic is notoriously bad. Leave South Beach 3 hours before kickoff.
  * Fan Hubs: Wynwood Walls, South Beach (Ocean Drive).

- Houston (NRG Stadium)
  * Location: 7 miles from Downtown.
  * Transport: METRORail Red Line to NRG Park Station.
  * Fan Hubs: Pitch 25 (Soccer Beer Park), Downtown.

- Dallas (AT&T Stadium)
  * Location: Arlington, TX (20 miles from Dallas).
  * Transport: NO TRAIN. Official Fan Shuttles from downtown are mandatory.
  * Warning: Arlington is a transport desert. Do not rely on finding a taxi after the game.
  * Fan Hubs: Texas Live! (Arlington), Deep Ellum (Dallas).

- Kansas City (Arrowhead)
  * Location: Truman Sports Complex (9 miles from downtown).
  * Transport: Z-Trip or Bus. Tailgating capital of the world.
  * Fan Hubs: Power & Light District (Huge watch parties).

- Seattle (Lumen Field)
  * Location: SoDo District (1 mile from downtown).
  * Transport: Walkable from Pioneer Square. Light Rail to Stadium Station.
  * Fan Hubs: Pioneer Square (March to the Match tradition).

- San Francisco Bay Area (Levi's Stadium)
  * Location: Santa Clara (45 miles south of SF).
  * Transport: Caltrain from SF to Mountain View -> VTA Light Rail.
  * Warning: It takes 2 hours from San Francisco. Stay in San Jose if you want to be close.
  * Fan Hubs: San Pedro Square Market (San Jose), Union Square (SF).

- Los Angeles (SoFi Stadium)
  * Location: Inglewood (12 miles from Downtown).
  * Transport: Metro K Line + Shuttle.
  * Fan Hubs: LA Live (Downtown), Santa Monica Pier.

[CANADA]
- Toronto (BMO Field): 2 miles from downtown. GO Train to Exhibition. Vibrant nightlife on King St West.
- Vancouver (BC Place): Downtown. Skytrain. Beautiful waterfront stadium.

[MEXICO]
- Mexico City (Estadio Azteca): 10 miles from center. Tren Ligero. High altitude warning.
- Guadalajara (Estadio Akron): 10 miles from center. Bus. Tequila capital.
- Monterrey (Estadio BBVA): 13 miles from center. Stunning mountain views.
